\spell{Puppet Show}% Name
  {Detailed, Duplicated}% Enhancements
  {Wane}% Action
  {Fire, Air}% Spheres
  {\roll{Wits}{Vigilance}}% Resist with
  {As the caster tells a tale with shadow puppets, each one stands and begins to move -- \arabic{spellTargets} in total}% Description
  {They play out their roles, with only a couple of simple actions which they form on repeat.
  They may annoy people by limiting their vision (inflicting a -\arabic{spellPlusOne}~Penalty) or confuse people by imitating the shadowy forms of real things.

  Bright lights damage them, by they remain immortal in darkness.}
